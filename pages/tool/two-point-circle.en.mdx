# Two-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Two-Point Circle' button='Toolbar'  buttonIcon='button-two-point-circle'/>

You can create a circle by specifying its diameter. 

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-two-point-circle" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click to set the starting point of the diameter.
3. Move the cursor to adjust the size of the circle, and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical / Horizontal:** Changes the inclination relative to the construction plane.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Right-click to confirm.
5. The circle is created.
