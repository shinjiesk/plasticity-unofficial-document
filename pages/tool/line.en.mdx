# Line

<Reference shortcut="Shift + A" command="Line" button="Toolbar"   buttonIcon='button-line'/>

You can create a series of connected line segments. 

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-line" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click to set the starting point of the line segment.
3. Click at the angle of the line segment to create a series of line segments.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>Ctrl + Z</kbd> **Undo:** Cancels the last click.
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. After clicking the last point, right-click to confirm.
5. The line segments are created.
