# Three Point Rectangle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three Point Rectangle' button='Toolbar'  buttonIcon='button-three-point-rectangle'/>

You can create a rectangle by specifying three points.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-rectangle" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click a corner of the rectangle.
3. Click another point of the rectangle.
4. Move the cursor to adjust the size, and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
3. Right-click to confirm.
5. The rectangle is created.
