# Center Rectangle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Center Rectangle' button='Toolbar' buttonIcon='button-center-rectangle'/>

You can create a rectangle by specifying its center point.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-center-rectangle" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click to specify the center point of the rectangle.
3. Move the cursor to adjust the size, and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Allows direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>C</kbd> **Center / Corner:** Switches to Corner Rectangle.
      - <kbd>S</kbd> **Square:** Restricts to a square shape.
3. Right-click to confirm.
5. The rectangle is created.
