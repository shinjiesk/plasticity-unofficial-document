# Three-Point Arc

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Arc' button='Toolbar'  buttonIcon='button-three-point-arc'/>

You can create an arc by specifying three points on its circumference.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-arc" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click at the first position on the circumference of the arc.
3. Move the cursor and click at the second point on the circumference.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Move the cursor and click at the third point on the circumference.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
5. The arc is created.
