# Tangent Arc

<Reference shortcut="" command="Tangent Arc" button="Toolbar"  buttonIcon='button-tangent-ark'/>

You can create an arc tangent to a curve.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-tangent-ark" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click the curve that the arc will be tangent to.
3. Move the cursor to adjust the size of the arc, and click to confirm.
    - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
        - <kbd>D</kbd> **Flip direction:** Reverses the direction of the arc.
        - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
6. The arc is created.
