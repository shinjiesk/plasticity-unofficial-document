# Center Point Arc

<Reference shortcut='' command='Center Point Arc' button='Toolbar' buttonIcon='button-center-point-arc'/>

You can create an arc by specifying the center and two points on the circumference.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-center-point-arc" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click at the center of the arc.
3. Move the cursor and click at the first position of the arc.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Allows direct input of dimensions. For details, see [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
4. Move the cursor to adjust the size of the arc and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Please refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/<kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrain to X, Y, Z axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
5. The arc is created.
