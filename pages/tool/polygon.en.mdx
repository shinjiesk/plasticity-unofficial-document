# Regular Polygon

<Reference shortcut='' command='Polygon' button='Toolbar' buttonIcon='button-regular-polygon'/>

You can create a polygon.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-regular-polygon" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click at the center of the polygon.
3. Move the cursor to adjust the size, and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](/tool/sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>Shift + Scroll Up</kbd> **Add a Vertex:** Increases the number of vertices of the polygon.
      - <kbd>Shift + Scroll Down</kbd> **Subtract a Vertex:** Decreases the number of vertices of the polygon.
      - <kbd>Up Arrow Key</kbd> Increases the number of vertices of the polygon.
      - <kbd>Down Arrow Key</kbd> Decreases the number of vertices of the polygon.
      - <kbd>V</kbd> **Toggle vertical / horizontal:** Changes the inclination relative to the construction plane.
      - <kbd>C</kbd> **Toggle circumscribed:** Switches between circumscribed and inscribed.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](/tool/sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/<kbd>Y</kbd> **X/Y-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y axis](/tool/sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Right-click to confirm.
5. The polygon is created.
