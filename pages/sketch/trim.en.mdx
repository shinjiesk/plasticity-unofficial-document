# Trim

<Reference shortcut="T" command="Trim" button="Toolbar"  buttonIcon='button-trim'/>

Remove specified segments from planar or 3D Curves.

## Basic Usage

1. Press <kbd>T</kbd>.
2. Click on the segment you want to trim.
3. The segment is removed.
