# Project Body Body [#project-body-body]

<Reference shortcut="I" command="Project Body Body" button="Right of the command bar"  buttonIcon='button-project'/>

Create a new Curve representing the intersection line between two Solids or Sheets.

## Basic Usage

1. Select two Solids or Sheets.
2. Press <kbd>I</kbd> or click the button.
3. A Curve representing the intersection line will be created.
